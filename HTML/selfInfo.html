14/06/1999

A date that has nothing intresting for most of you. For me? It's the day I was born. I poped out around 8 o' clock (AM that is). Such a cutie! Blue eyes, round cheecks, an nice round bum! My parts were as happy as they could've ever been. My broter? Well, he had someone to annoy so I guess he was happy to :D!

1999-2003

Ah, 't is the time in which many things happen but we don't remember any of it. We learn to brabble and eventually speak, we start to learn how to read, write, walk, run, bike(with sidewheels) and so much more!
As said before, I don't remember much from that time. Luckily, a lot got recorded an we've made them into our own DVD's with me and my brother as the stars! "Robbeke & Moutje's adventures!". They're worth watching believe me! Whilst watching them, I realized that Robbe and I didn't really got along all that well. I can assure you, now he's one of my best buds!

2003-2006

The time we went to 'school' for the first time. Me and my fellow todlers, having a blast, (p)laying around, and honing our everyday skills. Maybe a first 'lover'? I'm using quite a lot of '' and that's because it wasn't really school, not as we know it now at least and I didn't even know what loe really ment. It was cool to have a girlfriend ;). Still, they were some good times!

2006-2012

Primary school. You remember about 2 articles ago hen I was talking about learning how te read and write? Well this is were we all did it! The real deal. I don't know about the other schools but our school worked with a niveau of reading and writing and you had to reach a certain level in a specific grade.I remeber that I was extremely happy when I finally reached level 9!
Here again, love wasn't really real. (At a certain point I had 12 girlfriends at the same time, they knew and ddn't mind, that's how real it was!) The biggest thing was making friends for "life". How naive we were to think we would never lose eachother. In 6 th grade we had these 'big tests' to prepare ourselves for secondary school and let me tell you, I bossed em!
Overall a fun time!

2012-2017

WATCH OUT! We belong to the big boys now! That how we felt at the end of the 6 th grade of primary school. Then you enter secondary school and suddeny realise that you are the smalles, the youngest again. 6 years of survival, fighting the teachers because they sent way to much tests our way, fight the bullies, fight your friends when they prank you, ... (with fight, I don't mean litteraly hit someone!) to realize in the 6 th year that the teachers aren't always the bad ones and that all by all, it wasn't as hard as you remeber it was. I've got to say, I was really tired of secondary school quite quickly. Normally it's something you start to hear around the 5 th grade but I already wanted to quit around the 2 nd one. I don't know why but I didn't like secondary at all. All these strict rules, no freedom, you had to listen to whatever the teacher had to say and you were clearly underneath the. They had the power and weren't always respectful. Also a heartbreak has played a big part in there as well (and yes this time it was actually love!). I was happy when I graduated. Onto the next part in life!

2017-*

Here we are. The today. I was stocked to start this year. New school, new friends, courses that actually interest me instead of e.g. religion in secondary school. I hated going to school but now? I don't want to miss a lecture! I enjoy it way better. Goofing around with the boys, studieng somthing I actually enjoy, the freedom!
